Choosing the Right Agency

Red Flags

  • "Guaranteed #1 ranking" (nobody can guarantee this)
  • No examples / cases studies
  • Focus only on link building (one part of SEO)
  • No detailed reporting

Good Signs

  • Clear methodology and timeline
  • Reference clients you can contact
  • Monthly reports with real metrics (impressions, clicks, positions)
  • Flexible contract (exit if not satisfied)
