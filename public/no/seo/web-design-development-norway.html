Deep Dive

Design and Development Together

The gap between design and development is where most web projects lose quality. When designers hand off Figma files to separate developers, something is always lost in translation. devndespro eliminates this gap — one studio that designs in Figma and builds in React, with the same person making decisions at every stage.

The Gap Between Design and Development

The most common failure mode in Norwegian web projects is the gap between design and development. A design agency produces beautiful Figma files that a separate development agency builds imperfectly because developers are interpreting design decisions without understanding the intent behind them. The finished product is always a compromise.

Norwegian Market Considerations

Web design and development for the Norwegian market requires specific knowledge that generic agencies lack. GDPR compliance must be built into the architecture. Norwegian SEO requires understanding of how Norwegians actually search. Payment systems like Vipps must be integrated correctly. And bilingual Norwegian and English delivery must be implemented with proper hreflang configuration.
