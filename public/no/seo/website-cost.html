What Does a Website Cost?

Website cost depends on scope, technology, and complexity. Here is an honest guide to what different types of projects typically cost at devndespro — with fixed-price delivery and estimates provided within 24 hours of your brief.

Business Website (Norway)

A standard Norwegian business website — 5–10 pages, React/Next.js, Figma design, Norwegian SEO, GDPR compliance — typically delivers in 3–5 weeks. Pricing starts from NOK 25,000 for straightforward projects.

Web Application (Norway/USA)

A web application with user authentication, database-driven content, and backend APIs typically takes 8–16 weeks and is priced based on feature scope. Kontakt us for a detailed estimate specific to your requirements.

Small Business Website

For small businesses that need a professional online presence — 3–5 pages, contact form, Google Maps, local SEO — we offer a streamlined package with fast delivery and a clear fixed price.
