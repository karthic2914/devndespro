Mobile App Development for European Businesses

European businesses building mobile products need apps that meet GDPR requirements, support multiple European languages, and perform reliably across diverse Android and iOS device ecosystems. React Native delivers all of this from a single codebase — significantly reducing development cost without sacrificing quality.

Why React Native for European Mobile Apps

European businesses building mobile applications face a clear choice: native iOS and Android development at twice the cost, or React Native delivering both platforms from one codebase. For most European business applications React Native is the right answer delivering near-native performance with significant cost savings.

European App Store Compliance

Apps distributed in Europe must meet both Apple App Store and Google Play requirements as well as GDPR data handling standards. We configure every European mobile application with GDPR-compliant analytics, proper consent flows, data minimisation principles, and the documentation required for EU app store approval.

Types of European Apps We Build

  • Customer loyalty and retail engagement apps
  • Healthcare and wellness applications with GDPR health data compliance
  • Tourism and travel companion applications
  • Educational and training apps for European institutions
  • Fintech apps with PSD2 and open banking integrations
