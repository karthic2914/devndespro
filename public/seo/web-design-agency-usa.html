Project Scope and Timeline Expectations

For most USA clients, speed and clarity are as important as design quality. We define scope in phases so your team can launch core pages first, then expand. A typical project starts with discovery and content mapping, then UX direction, then visual and technical implementation. This phased approach avoids expensive rework and gives decision-makers predictable checkpoints.

Common timeline ranges are 3 to 5 weeks for service websites, 6 to 10 weeks for larger marketing websites, and 8 to 16 weeks for complex web applications that require custom workflows or integrations. Delivery speed depends heavily on how quickly content, approvals, and technical access are provided.
